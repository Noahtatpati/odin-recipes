Lasagna

Description

A rich, cheesy Italian classic layered with meat sauce and pasta.

Perfect comfort food for dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook beef
  3. Add sauce
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. Bake
